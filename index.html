CoderCM

Learning to code became fun

The mission

The mission of CoderCM is to provide children with a fun and engaging way to learn programming. We believe that programming should be accessible to everyone, and we are committed to making it as easy as possible for children to learn the fundamentals of programming.

How we do it

We use the drag-and-drop interface Alice to make it easy for children to learn programming. Our courses are designed to be interactive and engaging, with a focus on making learning fun and enjoyable.

Who we are

I'm a third year student-athlete at UNC Chapel Hill who is always looking for fun projects to develop my skills as an aspiring software developer. Drag-and-drop software is what originally got me into coding, and I would love to give the next generation of students the same opportunity to learn and grow in a way that is both fun and engaging. This is why I'm developing CoderCM, an online interactive course that teaches the fundamentals of programming to children. If you have any questions, I would love to hear from you!
